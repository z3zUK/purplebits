50 SHADES OF PURPLE

Like purple? This is the site for you.

All the purples under the sun* are here for your viewing pleasure, and to assist with creating colour palettes.

* Possibly a slight exaggeration.

↓

Purple refers to a variety of colors with a hue between red and blue.

Throughout history, it has long been connected to royalty, as the rarity and costs of purple dyes prohibited its use among commoners. It's also associated with magic, mystery, spirituality, and piety. Purple was popular with 1960s psychedelia too.

Scroll down to see some of the most popular purple tones, with their hex values. Use these to plan your purple colour palettes.
